About Us

We believe when families embrace science and learn about the world together, they raise curious kids who become lifelong learners.

We are a custom content creation and editorial services firm specializing in kids nonfiction.

Each program we create—whether an engaging book series, an amazing audio experience, or a robust digital platform—is designed to attract and retain a community of curious kids and their families. So whether you're looking to expand into the nonfiction space or maximize your current offerings, WonderLab Group can help you create smart, well-crafted, and successful products that build off of one another, each platform driving interested consumers to the next, culminating in a cohesive and impactful program.
